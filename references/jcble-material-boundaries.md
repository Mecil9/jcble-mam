## Classification

Use four levels:

- Public-safe: approved company intro, public product copy, public certificates, public cases.
- Internal: business plans, operating reviews, sales tasks, internal strategy.
- Restricted: pricing, contracts, client projects, financial summaries, delivery details.
- Forbidden: payroll, equity, bank accounts, API keys, credentials, personal resumes, raw finance tables, confidential customer details.

## Evidence Rules

- Cite source file path and date/version for every key business, product, customer, certification, price, or market claim.
- Separate fact, interpretation, and recommended action.
- Highlight stale or conflicting documents instead of silently choosing one.
- Verify current external facts, market numbers, regulations, product behavior, and policy claims with current sources.
- Keep new outputs separated from original historical documents.

## External-Facing Rules

For websites, social posts, product brochures, sales decks, public case studies, overseas outreach, and public images:

- Use only public-safe or explicitly approved source material.
- Do not expose contract amounts, customer private details, payroll, equity, bank information, or internal financial worksheets.
- Do not imply guaranteed results unless supported by approved evidence.
- Do not represent generated images as real field photos, real customer scenes, or real product photos.
- Require human approval before publication or email sending.
