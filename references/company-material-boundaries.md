# Company Material Boundaries

Use local evidence before planning, writing, publishing, or sending company-facing materials.

## Recommended Entry Files

Adapt these paths to the target company workspace:

- `COMPANY_WORKSPACE/AGENTS.md` or equivalent workspace instructions.
- `COMPANY_WORKSPACE/00.company-material-index.md` or equivalent company-material index.
- `COMPANY_KNOWLEDGE_BASE/00.knowledge-index.md` or equivalent knowledge-base index.
- `COMPANY_MARKETING_WORKSPACE/00.agent-team-index.md` when an Agent team has already been created.

If these files do not exist, create a small index before large content work. The index should map source folders to usage rules and owner approvals.

## Typical Material Areas

| Area | Primary Use | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Company profile and pitch materials | Company introduction, investor story, sales narrative | Verify latest approved version before external use |
| Product materials | Product manuals, specifications, certificates, screenshots, product images | Trace product parameters, certificates, and screenshots |
| Solution and proposal materials | Industry solutions, client proposals, case studies, technical proposals | Separate reusable public wording from client-specific material |
| Marketing and sales materials | Website copy, brochures, social posts, price sheets, sales enablement | Treat pricing and private sales notes as restricted by default |
| Operations and planning materials | Operating reviews, annual plans, OKRs, internal dashboards | Use for internal planning; sanitize before external use |
| Project and delivery materials | Real projects, bids, delivery records, support notes | Review customer names, site details, amounts, and commitments |
| Legal and compliance materials | Contracts, NDAs, certificates, licenses, compliance policies | Use only for internal verification unless approved |
| Public website staging | Draft public pages, product pages, case pages, lead forms | Suitable for public content after review |

## Classification

Use four levels:

- Public-safe: approved company introduction, approved product copy, public certificates, public case studies.
- Internal: operating plans, strategy, dashboards, OKRs, meeting notes, internal analysis.
- Restricted: pricing, contracts, client projects, financial summaries, delivery details, sales pipeline.
- Forbidden: payroll, equity, bank accounts, API keys, credentials, personal resumes, raw finance tables, confidential customer details.

## Evidence Rules

- Cite source file path and date/version for every key business, product, customer, certification, price, or market claim.
- Separate fact, interpretation, and recommended action.
- Highlight stale or conflicting documents instead of silently choosing one.
- Verify current external facts, market numbers, regulations, product behavior, and policy claims with current sources.
- Keep new outputs separated from original historical documents.

## External-Facing Rules

For websites, social posts, product brochures, sales decks, public case studies, overseas outreach, and public visuals:

- Use only public-safe or explicitly approved source material.
- Do not expose contract amounts, private customer details, payroll, equity, bank information, credentials, or internal financial worksheets.
- Do not imply guaranteed results unless supported by approved evidence.
- Do not represent generated visuals as real field photos, real customer scenes, or real product photos.
- Require human approval before publication, list export, external pricing, or email sending.
